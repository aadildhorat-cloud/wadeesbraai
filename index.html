Premium Braai Catering in Emmerentia, Johannesburg

Wadee's Braai Catering is a trusted supplier of premium marinated meats and braai catering services in Emmerentia, Johannesburg. We specialize in lamb chops, steaks (T-bone, fillet, min), wors, kebabs (beef, mutton, chicken), chicken tikka, spicy wings, sausages, patties, and skewers. All our products are HALAAL certified by SANHA (S-1569).

Our Product Categories

  • Premium Steaks & Chops: Lamb chops, min steak, T-bone steak (hot), aged fillet steak, beef rashers.
  • Kebabs & Skewers: Gourmet chicken kebabs, mutton kebabs, beef kebabs, skewers.
  • Wors & Sausages: Traditional wors, mutton dhania sausages, dhania sausages (beef), sweet chilli sausages, garlic polony.
  • Chicken Specialties: Chicken tikka, spicy wings, chicken fillet.
  • Patties: Beef patties (6 in a pack), mutton patties (6).

Frequently Asked Questions About Wadee's Braai Catering

What products does Wadee's Braai Catering offer?

We offer premium marinated meats including lamb chops, steaks (T-bone, fillet, min), wors, kebabs (beef, mutton, chicken), chicken tikka, spicy wings, sausages, patties, and skewers. All our products are HALAAL certified.

Are your products HALAAL certified?

Yes! All our products are HALAAL certified by SANHA (South African National Halaal Authority), certification number S-1569.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM, and Saturday: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you deliver or is it collection only?

We offer both collection from our Emmerentia location and delivery options. Please contact us via WhatsApp or phone to arrange delivery for your order.
